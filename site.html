World of Solitaire has over 50 solitaire games, including Spider, Klondike, FreeCell and Pyramid.
A 100% free online solitaire game with undo support, multiple decks, statistics, custom backgrounds and more.
Created with HTML5 and JavaScript, this online website works everywhere!

My name is Robert and I created World of Solitaire back in 2007.
My goal was to create a free, high quality, no hassle site to play solitaire on.
I hoped for 30 happy players each month. Today, over 250,000 people come to the site every day!
It is absolutely thrilling to me to know that so many people enjoy what I have created.
I truly hope that you all continue to enjoy playing here for many years to come!
